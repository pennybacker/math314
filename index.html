MATH 314: Linear Algebra with Applications (Fall 2016)

Prof. Matthew Pennybacker

Overview

This course is meant as an application-based introduction to linear algebra. We will study linear systems of equations and their properties in order to better understand the nature of vector spaces and operators on those spaces. Among other topics, we will encounter material on linear transformations, matrices, eigenvalues and eigenvectors, and matrix decompositions. Please consult the syllabus for more information.
